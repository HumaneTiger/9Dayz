1. Select a square in the game world

2a. Select the object you want to place

2b. Beam character to that place

3. Place it in the selected square

The new object behaves like any pre-placed object. If you place it in direct range of the character, its Card will spawn instantly.

If you place it anywhere else, you have to move your character close to that place to make its Card spawn.

Please note: Only objects placed next to or on a street will be seen by the character and spawn.

9 Dayz is a harsh und unforgiving survival game. Every action you take and every decision you make count – either instantly or in a longer term. This is not a game you can just rush into.

If you are a new player, please make sure to prepare yourself by choosing the tutorial option.

Your character will continuously walk on the edge of the abyss, and his death is almost inevitable during your first attempts. Despair, good and bad luck, relief and finally the successful escape are all part of the many experiences and feelings you will have in the following 9 Dayz.

Tutorial - Fighting Enemies

When your character Attacks a zombie, other zombies in that area will join the battle. Avoid fighting against more than one Zombie in the beginning!

There is no need to hurry here - during battles the in-game time is paused.

Lure single zombies into a fight (chance is 50:50), to make the battle easier. Zombies will attack you first, when you walk right into them.

Once the battle starts, all your inventory items turn into a Battle Card deck. Each item now has orange Damage and blue Protection values. Make sure you have enough items at hand before starting a fight.

Drag a Card over a Zombie to deal damage and to shield yourself. Each action consumes one Action Point. The item will permanently be removed from your inventory.

Good luck!
