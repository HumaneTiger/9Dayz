Day 1

Tutorial - Fighting Enemies

When your character Attacks a zombie, other zombies in that area will join the battle. Avoid fighting against more than one Zombie in the beginning!

There is no need to hurry here - during battles the in-game time is paused.

Lure single zombies into a fight (chance is 50:50), to make the battle easier. Zombies will attack you first, when you walk right into them.

Once the battle starts, all your inventory items turn into a Battle Card deck. Each item now has orange Damage and blue Protection values. Make sure you have enough items at hand before starting a fight.

Drag a Card over a Zombie to deal damage and to shield yourself. Each action consumes one Action Point. Items will permanently be removed from your inventory so choose them wisely. Each time a weapon deals damage, its durability gets reduced. You can improve and repair your equiped weapons outside of battles.
